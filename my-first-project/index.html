NEUROTIC BACTERIA

Scientific Name:

NEUROTIC BACTERIA

Discovered by / at:

Team XM, December 1901

Family:

Upon seperation from the rock the creature seems lost and moves non-stop. The team of scientists is trying to discover its life time span. It was the only creature found on the surface at the moment. Through experiments, the creature seems to be 1000000 years old, formed from a star collision all those years ago.

Habitat:

It has adapted to living on galaxy rocks, in cold, solid environments.

Appearance:

It's sporatic leg movement led to its name. It has lots of energy and glides quickly across the dish's surface.

Key Characteristics:

  • Changes color.
  • Moves sporadically.
  • Always hungry.

What do we know so far about neurotic bacteria? In 1901, a galaxy rock landed on earth and scientists immidiately took to examine any sign of life. They scratched off some sample material from its surface and brought it back to the lab. To their astonishment they looked through the microscope and found a moving creature. They began to wonder... What can it do? How does it evolve? Can it grow...?
